Privacy & Security

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

We take your privacy seriously. Your personal data, including your profile, contact information, and activity, is stored securely and is never shared with third parties without your consent.

What We Collect

  • Your name, email, and profile information
  • Your form submissions and login activity
  • Any uploaded files (e.g., profile pictures, documents)

How We Use It

  • To provide access to features based on your role
  • To personalize your experience
  • To maintain system security

Your Control

  • You can update your profile information at any time
  • You can request data deletion through account settings
  • You may view, edit, or request removal of your data

If you have any questions or concerns about how your data is handled, please contact the site administrator or support.
